About Us

The Health & Safety Authority (HSA) is the governing body overseeing professional standards, clinical safety, and staff accreditation at Maple Hospital. We are composed of licensed healthcare professionals, senior administrators, and certified trainers who ensure all staff and volunteers adhere to the highest standards of virtual care.
